Traditions


Besikke salu

Ceremony of placing the baby in the cradle for the first time. The women of the village who are invited to the cradle come with their hair and clothing.

Tusau kesu

Cutting the bonds on the childs feet so that his future path in life is easy and bright.

Atqa otyrgyzu

A party held by parents for the joy of riding a horse for the first time.

Zheti ata

Mandatory knowledge of the names of your ancestors in the male line up to the seventh generation.

Qyrqynan shygaru

A ritual performed forty days after the birth of a baby.

Shashu

It is a tradition to shower people with candies and coins during joyful moments.
