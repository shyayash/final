Our national clothing


Taqia

Skullcap, a traditional male and female headdress of hemispherical, pointed shape

Bes bilezik

It is a type of fashionable jewelry, consisting of bracelets and rings connected by a chain, worn on two or three fingers at a time.

Kimeshek

Kazakh headdress of married women made of thin white cloth, tightly fitting the head, covering the neck, chest, shoulders and back.

Shashbau

A decorative item worn by girls in their hair.

Qamzol

Men's clothing (military or court), sewn in the waist, knee-length, sometimes sleeveless, with a standing or lagging collar, worn under the justokor.

Syrgalar

Kazakh earrings.

Qalpaq

A conical hat made of felt or other soft material.

Saukele

high cone-shaped Kazakh headdress, about 70 centimeters high, decorated with silver and gold coins, pearls and corals, one of the ancient headdresses used by Kazakhs until the end of the XIX century.

Shapan

Kazakh ancient and traditional clothes.
